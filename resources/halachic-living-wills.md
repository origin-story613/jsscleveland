Setting up your Halachic Living Will is as easy as 1‑2‑3:

1. **Choose your proxy and rabbi** — the people you trust to make decisions on your behalf.
2. **Download or request the Halachic Living Will** below, and fill it out.
3. **Contact us** to receive a free EMES wallet card and a sleeve to attach it to your license or ID.

## Register your living will

Please download the Halachic Living Will, fill it out and sign it, then return it to Rabbi Nissim Abrin at [Rabbi.nabrin@jsscleveland.com](mailto:Rabbi.nabrin@jsscleveland.com).
