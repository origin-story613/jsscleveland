The Chevra Kadisha of Greater Cleveland maintains the highest level of respect and dignity (kavod hames) in caring for all Jewish people at the time of a death. We are a volunteer organization offering burial preparation to any Jewish person, regardless of denomination or observance.

We are available to assist families with sensitivity and expertise with any questions regarding an imminent or recent death. Before making funeral arrangements, it is helpful to understand the basic principles and practices concerning death and mourning to which Judaism subscribes.

## Our mission

Throughout Jewish history, caring for the deceased has been considered one of the greatest mitzvot — a kindness that can never be repaid, since the one who receives it can offer nothing in return. The members of the Chevra Kadisha are chosen for their character, integrity, and devotion to Jewish tradition, and are on call to ensure that every Jewish person is prepared for burial according to Jewish law and custom. Men care for men, women care for women, Jew cares for fellow Jew.
